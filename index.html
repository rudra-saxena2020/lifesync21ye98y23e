    About LifeSync

    LifeSync is a real-time companion app designed especially for senior citizens who live alone. With a focus on safety, health, and emotional connection, LifeSync bridges the gap between independence and support. From instant SOS alerts to seamless video calls and health tracking, it ensures peace of mind for both elders and their families. Our mission is to empower seniors to live confidently while staying connected with their loved ones and caregivers.
